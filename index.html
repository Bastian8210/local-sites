Our story

Honest burgers,
retro soul

Retro Burger started with one simple idea: do the classics properly. No shortcuts, no fuss — just quality meat, fresh toppings and buns toasted to order. Over the past year we've become a go-to spot in Vejle for hungry locals who know a good burger when they taste one.

Whether you're grabbing a quick takeaway, settling in for a dine-in feast or getting it delivered to your door, every order is built fresh and stacked with care. Pull up a stool — the grill's already hot.
